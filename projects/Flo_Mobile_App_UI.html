Project Overview

For this project, I was to come up with an idea for a music app and create UI mockups for it. The concept behind the app is a music app that also functions as a Pomodoro Technique timer, which is a technique that is often used for studying or other tasks that require concentration. Users would be able to select songs and artists that the app would use when selecting songs to play during the selected time frame. The app would then select music related to these songs and artists and create a playlist that fits the length of time selected by the user. The logo and icons were made using Illustrator, and the rest was done in Photoshop.
