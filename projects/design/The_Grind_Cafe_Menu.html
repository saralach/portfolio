Project Overview

This project required me to develop a fictional company as well as a logo and a menu for that company. The company idea I came up with was "The Grind", a cafe that is designed to be a study spot for high school and college students. With this idea in mind, I selected food items that would be appealing to this demographic and also contain ingredients that promote focus and concentration. I also green as the primary color because in color psychology it is known to promote concentration as well. I also used earth tones to add a feel of clean and natural food.
