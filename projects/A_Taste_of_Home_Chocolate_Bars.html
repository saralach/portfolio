Project Overview

For this project, I was assigned to create chocolate bar designs inspired by a particular artist or designer. The designer I was assigned was Do Ho Suh. A common theme of Suh's work is a feeling of displacement from one's culture and one's home. With that in mind, I chose to create "A Taste of Home", a brand of chocolate bars with flavors from around the world, since such a product could help ease such feelings of displacement when one is far away from their home and their culture. I chose to use brightly-colored line drawings to emulate the colors and the stitchwork that can be found on many of Suh's fabric structures.
