WILD Email Design

Timeline

February – March 2024

Software & Tools

  • Photoshop

Project Overview

For this project, I came up with an apparel brand that is designed for outdoor enthusiasts and created a promotional email design for the beginning of the spring season. I used Photoshop's slice feature so each section of the email can link to a corresponding webpage. Since this is a fictional company, the links within the email (see below) are linking to a similar brand to demonstrate the capability.
