Project Overview

For this project, the Carroll University Computer Science and Information Technology departments requested that I create sticker and notepad designs to be used for promotional materials for the departments.

For both designs, I wanted to keep it clean and simple and ensure brand recognition. Therefore, I chose to center the designs around typical Carroll logos and colors, and add small details to allude to the Computer Science and Information Technology departments. For the sticker design, I added binary (0s and 1s) in a monospaced font to encorporate the technology aspect.

In addition to the designs, I helped with communication with the printer for the stickers to make sure they had the types of files they needed and that the dieline was set up and ready for printing and cutting.
